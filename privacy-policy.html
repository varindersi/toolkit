Introduction

At Color Design Toolkit, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and tools.

Data Collection

We collect minimal data to provide and improve our services:

  • Color preferences and palettes you create (stored locally in your browser)
  • Basic usage statistics (anonymized and aggregated)
  • Contact information (only when you choose to reach out to us)

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Provide and maintain our color tools
  • Improve user experience and tool functionality
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our services

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • All color data is processed locally in your browser
  • We use secure HTTPS connections
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited data collection and retention

Cookies and Local Storage

We use:

  • Essential cookies for basic functionality
  • Local storage to save your color preferences
  • Analytics cookies (optional) to improve our services

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics (optional)
  • Cloudflare (for security and performance)
  • Social media platforms (when you choose to share)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Receive your data in a portable format
